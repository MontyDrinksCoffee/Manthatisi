Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Lovers & Friends Lifestyle Chill-out.

What is Lovers & Friends Lifestyle Chill-out?

Lovers & Friends Lifestyle Chill-out is a premium lifestyle event hosted at Manthatisi Self-Catering Units. It is an all-VIP inclusive experience offering good food, fashionable lifestyle, great people, and great vibes at one of the most exquisite venues in Maseru.

Is it a paying event and how do I purchase a ticket?

Yes, this is a paying event. Payments can be made via Mpesa, Ecocash, or cash. Please note that tickets are limited.

What is the age group and are there any restrictions?

The event is strictly for guests between the ages of 18 and 50 years. Valid identification may be required at the entrance.

Will food and drinks be allowed at the venue?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed. Quality food and drinks will be available for purchase at the venue.

Do I need to bring my own chair?

No. Lovers & Friends Lifestyle Chill-out is an all-VIP experience. Seating will be provided for every patron on the day of the event.
